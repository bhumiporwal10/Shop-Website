Premium Glass, Doors, and Hardware

At Laxmi Glass House (LGH), we specialize in high-quality glass and door solutions that enhance any space. In addition to our modern glass designs and elegant door systems, we offer a full range of essential hardware and furniture components, including screws, nails, hinges, door kits, holders, and adhesives. Based in Barisadri, Rajasthan, and led by Gajendra Porwal, LGH is renowned for its top-notch products and exceptional service. Whether you're redesigning your home or working on a new project, LGH provides reliable solutions for all your needs.
