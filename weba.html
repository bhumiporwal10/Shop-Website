Versatile Plywood Options

At Laxmi Glass House, we offer a variety of plywood to suit different needs and environments. Whether you're looking for durability in moist conditions or reliable materials for dry areas, our plywood selection covers it all.

Our Plywood Types:

Waterproof Plywood: Ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and outdoor furniture. Treated to withstand moisture and water exposure.

Semi-Waterproof Plywood: Great for furniture and cabinets in areas with moderate moisture. Balances durability and water resistance.

Non-Waterproof Plywood: Best for dry indoor environments. Suitable for general construction, furniture, and cabinetry projects.
